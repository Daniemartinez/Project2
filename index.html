Rust Prevention

Product Defintion
RP 913 A water-soluble, polymer-based rust preventer that mixes readily with water. The film left after evaporation is a thin polymer film which is easily removed. This product may be applied by spray or immersion and the work is to be stored indoors.
RP 611 A rust preventer designed primarily for in-process protection. It is easily dilluted in water and is formulated with the latest polymer technology. This compound is designed for 60-90 day storage.
RP 912 A rust preventer made with a blend of organic inhibitors. The protective coating is a thin, oily film which provides excellent protection for parts during extended indoor storage. Formulated for use on cast iron.
